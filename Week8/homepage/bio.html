OUR STORY


D’s Kitchen is a rock band from Bangalore, India. Formed in 2015, the band relies on simplicity and impactful, emotive songwriting to form a unique, yet effective sound. The band’s sound can be generally classified as rock, though it has elements of indie, blues, grunge and other genres, the use of which comes from the different musical journeys of the band’s members.
The band released its first album, Light Does Echo in January 2016 to positive reviews. The band released its second full-length release, This Side Of The Border, in August 2017. The album release will be accompanied by an extensive India tour.
The band also shot a live performance of the new album along with unreleased material, which was telecast on Kappa TV as part of their ‘Music Mojo’ program. The telecast will release on Youtube as well in the coming days. In the last few months the band has played at various venues in Bengaluru, Goa and Pondicherry to support the release.
The lineup consists of Rohit Kamath on vocals, Vaibhav Kumar and Himanshu Arora on guitars, Nipun Narayan on bass guitar, and Madhusudan Raman on drums.
